Learn in public

I'm a enthusiastic believer and evangelist of the benefits of open culture (open knowledge, open source, open data, ...), collaboration, and continuous learning, hence I love helping others (as much as I can) and the idea of learning in public.

And as if that wasn't enough... I also believe in karma 🫶🏼.

But things are proven by facts and not by words, so on this page I will be gathering references to these facts.

Communities​

I have been involved in technical communities & forums for more than ~20 years now, when I joined IRC-Hispano. Since then, I have contributed, co-founded or lead many others community initiatives, several of them became non-profits:

  • 2020 - today - Asociación Española de GeoVoluntariado (co-founder & president)
  • 2014 - today - GeoDevelopers.org (founder & community leader) & Esri Community
  • 2018 - 2022 - Asociación Tomelloso Tech (co-founder & president)
  • 2015 - 2016 - Iniciador Madrid & Iniciador Foundation (community leader & governing board)
  • 2014 - 2015 - Startup Weekend Madrid (organizer)
  • 2014 - 2014 - Comunidades Tecnológicas Madrid (member)
  • 2012 - 2012 - Google Developer Group (GDG) Spain (community leader)
  • 2010 - 2012 - Google Developer Group (GDG) Granada (co-founder & community leader)
  • 2010 - 2011 - Asociación de Webmasters de Málaga (co-founder & secretary)
  • 2009 - 2011 - Asociación de Webmasters de Granada (co-founder & president)
  • 2009 - 2011 - Iniciador Granada & Málaga (co-founder & community leader)
  • 2009 - 2010 - Free Software Office of the University of Granada (contributor)
  • 2004 - 2009 - ForosdelWeb.com (active user)
  • 2001 - 2003 - HackHispano (moderator)
  • 1999 - 2001 - IRC-Hispano (user & moderator)

In addition to all these, I'm have joined and participated in more communities than I can remember 😅 (mailing lists, meetups, Slack, Discord, Telegram, ... ).
